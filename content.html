Morning star:
The Morning Star is a bullish candlestick pattern that signals a potential reversal in a downtrend. It consists of three candles:
First Candle (Bearish - Red): A long red candlestick indicating strong selling pressure.
Second Candle (Small - Green or Red): A small-bodied candle (can be a Doji or Spinning Top) showing market indecision.
Third Candle (Bullish - Green): A strong green candlestick confirming a reversal and indicating buyers taking control.
Key Points:
Found at the bottom of a downtrend.
Signals a shift from bearish to bullish momentum.
Higher volume on the third candle strengthens the pattern.
Confirmation comes when the next candle continues to rise.
This pattern is useful for traders looking to identify potential buying opportunities.

Evening Star Candlestick Pattern Analysis

The Evening Star is a bearish reversal candlestick pattern that appears at the top of an uptrend and signals a potential shift from bullish to bearish momentum.

Pattern Composition:

  • First Candle (Bullish - Green): A long green candlestick showing strong buying pressure.
  • Second Candle (Small - Red or Green): A small-bodied candle (Doji or Spinning Top) indicating market indecision.
  • Third Candle (Bearish - Red): A strong red candlestick confirming a reversal as sellers take control.

Key Points:

  • Found at the top of an uptrend.
  • Indicates a shift from bullish to bearish sentiment.
  • The higher the volume on the third candle, the stronger the signal.
  • Confirmation occurs when the next candle continues to decline.
  • Used by traders to identify potential selling opportunities.

Trading Strategy:

  • Entry: After the third candle closes red, confirming the pattern.
  • Stop-Loss: Above the high of the second or third candle.
  • Take Profit: Based on support levels or risk-reward ratios.
